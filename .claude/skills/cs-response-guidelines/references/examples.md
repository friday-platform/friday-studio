<example>
Customer question: "I can't log in to Bucketlist"

## Issue Classification
Login Failure — Common, Tier 1

## Knowledge Base Sources
- [How do I Log In to Bucketlist?](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/how-do-i-log-in-to-bucketlist) — Step-by-step login instructions
- [Common Login Issues](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/common-login-issues) — Troubleshooting invalid credentials and SSO conflicts

## Similar Past Tickets
- **"Can't log in"**: Customer's company changed email domains; Bucketlist account still used the old email. Resolved by admin updating the email.

## Suggested Actions
1. Share the login KB article with the customer.
2. Confirm the customer is using the correct platform URL.
3. Ask if the issue persists in incognito mode or on a different device.
4. Escalate to Tier 2 if the account appears locked or deactivated.

---

## Recommended Response (Draft)

Hi [Customer Name],

Thanks for reaching out! Here's how to log in:

1. Go to **companyname.bucketlist.org**
2. Enter the **email address** your company registered you with
3. Enter your password and click **Log In**

If you see an "Invalid credentials" error, double-check you're using the exact email your admin set up. This can sometimes be browser/cache related — could you try in an incognito window or on a different device?

Full walkthrough: [How do I Log In to Bucketlist?](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/how-do-i-log-in-to-bucketlist)

Kind regards,
[Agent Name]
Bucketlist Support

<example>
Customer question: "I redeemed a gift card but never received it"

## Issue Classification
Gift Card Not Received — Common, Tier 1

## Knowledge Base Sources
- [I can't find my gift card](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/i-cant-find-my-gift-card/i-havent-received-my-redemption) — Troubleshooting missing redemptions, delivery timelines, and spam folder checks
- [What can I expect when redeeming points?](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/what-can-i-expect-when-redeeming-points) — Explains automated vs manually processed gift cards and delivery timelines

## Similar Past Tickets
- **"Gift card not received"**: Customer redeemed but email went to spam. Resolved by resending to same email.
- **"Lowes gift card not received"**: Manually processed card took 2 business days. Resolved after processing completed.

## Suggested Actions
1. Check the redemption in the system and confirm it was processed.
2. Resend the gift card to the customer's email.
3. Ask the customer to check their spam/junk folder.
4. If still not received, offer to resend to an alternate email address.
5. Escalate to Tier 2 if the redemption appears stuck or the vendor is unresponsive.

---

## Recommended Response (Draft)

Hi [Customer Name],

I'm sorry to hear you haven't received your gift card. I've resent it to your email. Please check your spam/junk folder and let me know if you do not receive it.

Some of our gift cards are automated and sent instantly, but others are processed manually by our team. Manually processed gift cards typically arrive within 1-3 business days.

If you still don't receive it, could you please provide an alternate email address? I'd be happy to resend it there instead.

Kind regards,
[Agent Name]
Bucketlist Support
</example>

<example>
Customer question: "Our Slack integration stopped posting recognitions"

## Issue Classification
Slack Integration Failure — Common, Tier 1 (re-add) / Tier 2 (persistent failure)

## Knowledge Base Sources
- [How to Add Slack](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/how-to-add-slack) — Setup guide including channel requirements and authorization

## Similar Past Tickets
- **"Slack integration removed?"**: Integration disappeared from channel. Cause unclear — possibly removed by a Slack admin.

## Suggested Actions
1. Share the Slack KB article and ask the customer to re-add the integration.
2. Confirm the target Slack channel is **public**.
3. Verify **user emails in Slack match** their Bucketlist emails.
4. Escalate to Tier 2 if it fails again after re-adding.

---

## Recommended Response (Draft)

Hi [Customer Name],

Thanks for reaching out. Slack integrations can sometimes disconnect. Here's how to reconnect:

1. In Bucketlist, click the **grey down arrow** in the top-right and select **Integrations**
2. Click **"Add to Slack"**
3. Select your Slack workspace and choose a **public channel**
4. Click **Authorize**

Make sure user emails in Slack match their Bucketlist emails — mismatches can prevent recognitions from posting.

Full guide: [How to Add Slack](https://support.bucketlistrewards.com/en/knowledge/how-to-add-slack)

Kind regards,
[Agent Name]
Bucketlist Support
</example>
